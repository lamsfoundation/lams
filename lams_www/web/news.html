Welcome to LAMS 4.5!

New features:

Improved collaborative tools

Introducing doKu

doKu is a collaborative real-time editor, allowing students to simultaneously create and edit a text document. Teachers can also view how the students interact and work together to create their work. doKu now supports images as well

New tool: Whiteboard

A graphical collaboration tool where students can simultaneously share images, drawings and text to create group posters.

Gallery Walks

Gallery Walks are active learning technique where students are encouraged to build on their knowledge about a topic by interacting with other students. Now LAMS implements gallery walks in Group Assessments, doKus and Whiteboards!

Author Learning Designs like never before

The new Authoring interface has been simplified and looks terrific and modern, making the creation of learning designs a breeze.

Improved Question Bank!

All your assessment questions in a single place! With better analytics to improve your questions' life cycle and assessment efficacy.

Learning Outcomes

Map your curriculum and learning outcomes to all activities in your learning design. Also assess students performance directly to the learning outcomes. 

New Teaching Strategies

Use learning design templates for dozens of teaching strategies.
